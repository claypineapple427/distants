Welcome Home

DISTANTS is being built as a calm, useful, and humane place for people who may need guidance, reassurance, reminders, resources, or simply a better way to find their next step.

Empty shelf: This front room will later hold the primary welcome message, short introduction, and first-action guidance.

Companion

This room is reserved for the DISTANTS Companion concept: gentle guidance, task support, reminders, and user-centered assistance.

Future contents: Companion greeting, user guidance, reminder support, and voice-friendly instructions.

Senior Shield

Senior Shield is intended to help identify benefits, deadlines, exemptions, expense drift, and practical protections for seniors and fixed-income users.

Future contents: benefit reminders, senior relief resources, fraud alerts, renewal checks, and financial stability notes.

LEVERAGE Library

LEVERAGE is the curated resource library: links, references, agencies, emergency resources, recovery tools, and trusted public information.

Future contents: CDC, NIH, FEMA, Red Cross, SAMHSA, NAMI, OSHA, university, and government resource links.

Notes / Records

This room will hold reusable notes, completed items, project records, logs, and preserved observations that may later become part of the DISTANTS blueprint.

Future contents: boilerplate corpus, project notes, completed tasks, reusable text, and indexed records.

Workshop / Operations

This is the back-office area for operational tools. Public users do not need this room. GWIZ, publishing, testing, enrollment, maintenance, and administrative utilities may live here.

Future contents: GWIZ, publishing queue, content intake, testing notes, administrative tools, and maintenance logs.

About DISTANTS

DISTANTS is a growing effort to preserve helpful guidance, reduce confusion, support continuity, and create a welcoming place for those who may need assistance.

Future contents: mission statement, doctrine, founders note, purpose summary, and public explanation.
